Provides an implementation of a skyline algorithm. The skyline algorithm for routes was shown in:
  1. F. Graf, H.-P. Kriegel, M. Renz, M. Schubert: PAROS: Pareto Optimal Route Selection, In Proceedings of the ACM International Conference on Management of Data (SIGMOD), Indianapolis, IN: 1199–1202, 2010.
  2. M. Schubert, M. Renz, H.-P. Kriegel Route skyline queries: a multi-preference path planning approach In Proceedings of the 26th International Conference on Data Engineering (ICDE), Long Beach, CA: 261–272, 2010.
